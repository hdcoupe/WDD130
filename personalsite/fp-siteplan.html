Target Audience

Anyone and eveyone is welcome to share and observe art that has been created, whether you are just looking or if you are considering purchasing. This site will expose you to art and allow you to see what art people have created.

Color Scheme

  • Red - E30505, I will use this for text links
  • Light Gray - DAD6D6, I will use this for the background

Site Goals

If you have wanted to experience art or show off your own art, this is the website for you, with this website you will be able to display your art to any who come across this website, it will be a great place to display and see art that people have created. This site will answer questions such as 'What art is allowed?' Or 'Why should I want my art here?' The main activity that will be on this site is to look and see art that has been created The site should be easy to read that will make people want to visit this site than other art sites.

Typography

Yanone Kaffeesatz for the main body. Dancing Script for titles of art work.

Site Name

Site name may be Henry's Art Collection, or Art Emporium.
