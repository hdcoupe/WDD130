About the Rivers

Our river adventures will get you excited like no other adventure. We have a wide variety of courses you can take. if you are an experienced rafter, you are more than welcome here. Anyone new to rafting can take our beginner courses and work their way up to becoming an experienced rafter. You can learn all about our rivers and courses here!

For our beginner rafters we offer a selection of courses where you can learn the basics of rafting, you will be taught how to wear a life vest properly, how to hold your oar and how to work as a team to create the best rafting experience for everyone. Once you have those basics learned we will take you out on the river and have a gentle current to teach you how to put the basic principles to work.
