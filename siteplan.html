Target Audience

Our river rafting adventures here in Florida is suited for everyone, whether you have experience rafting or not. We encourage everyone to come down and learn to love the adventure that is rafting. We have selections of rafting that will fit everyones comfort zone. Our white water rafting adventure progra, is perfect for a family vacation or a private expedition.

Scenarios

We have many different situations that you can raft in, if you are a beginner, we recommend you start at our beginner course where we take you down the river when there are no rapids, it's more like a lazy river where we can teach you the basics of steering the raft with your team. We also offer an intermediate level where a guide will take you down the river with the rapids ranging between class 3 and 4 this intermediate level has more obstructions in the water that you will need to avoid. And for you all rafting experts we have everything for you, we can take you on a private expedition with rapids that are class 4 and 5, you'll have a blast speeding down the river with a team of other experienced rafters working along side you. (Disclaimer Food is not provided by the tour, we will carry your food to the rest areas along the way.)
